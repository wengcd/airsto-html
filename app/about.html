Can I leave my Air Wick Plug In Diffuser on all the time?

The Air Wick plug in diffuser should not be used continuously. It is important to remember to always switch it off at the socket overnight, and not use for more than 15 hours at a time.

Can I alter the fragrance intensity of the Plug In Unit?

Yes, the Air Wick plug in diffuser provides very accurate fragrance control. On the top of the unit there is a revolving button control, which allows the consumer to increase or decrease the fragrance intensity. We recommend 1 for minimal fragrance intensity, and 5 for maximum.

How do I remove the diffuser refill from the unit?

Replace the fragrance bottle when empty by first removing the air freshening unit from the power outlet then firmly pulling the bottle down from unit.

How does the unit shut-off?

The diffuser unit shuts off when it is unplugged. Take care if there is any scented oil remaining. The product must be stored in a cool place in an upright position to avoid leakage. Do not place on any wood, polished or painted surfaces.

What fragrances are available?

The Air Wick Plug In Diffuser is available in several scents, with new fragrances constantly added to the collection. For a full list of fragrances, please visit : http://www.airwick.co.uk/plug-ins-single-plug-in
